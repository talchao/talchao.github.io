About

@talchao is a 3D artist specializing in creating Minecraft: Bedrock Edition models using Blockbench. With over two years of experience in Blockbench and more than four years of general game texturing experience, they have a deep passion for bringing their creations to life. As the creator of Minecraft Prom and a contributor to projects such as Silly Kits and Blockémon, talchao hopes to spread whimsy and fun through their creations — just as those who have inspired them have done.
